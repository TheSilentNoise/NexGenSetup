Internship Opportunities


What we Provide

Internships are an excellent opportunity for a student or young worker to solve a real life use case where they work either part time or full time at a company for a certain period of time. Most internships are work experience internships — essentially on-the-job training in a field that the student or young worker wants to learn more about. Iearn.tech connects an internship seeker to various internship opportunities and also provide guidance and support during the internship period.
