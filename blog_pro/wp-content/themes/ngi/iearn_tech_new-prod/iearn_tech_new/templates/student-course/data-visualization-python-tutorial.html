Project

Download the Walmart Sales dataset from kaggle using the below link -

https://www.kaggle.com/c/walmart-sales-forecasting/data

Read the file and load it into a data frame.

Plot the following using seaborn and plotly:

  1. Plot the distribution of Sales
  2. Plot the distribution of Store
  3. Plot the distribution of Store Type
  4. Plot the distribution of Temperature
  5. Plot the distribution of Fuel Price
  6. Plot the distribution of CPI
  7. Plot the distribution of Unemployment
  8. Plot the relation between Sales and CPI
  9. Plot the relation between Sales and Unemployment
  10. Plot the relation between Sales and Temperature
  11. Plot the relation between Sales and Type Store
  12. Plot the relation between Sales and Day of week
  13. Plot the relation between Sales and Month of year
  14. Plot the relation between Sales and Year
  15. Plot the relation between Sales and Day Type - Weekday, Weekend, Holiday and LongHoliday
  16. Plot the relation between Sales and Fuel price
